# PART B — KEYWORD INTELLIGENCE MATRIX

## 🧮 The Revenue Potential Score (RPS) Formula

```
RPS = (Monthly US Volume × CPC × Intent Multiplier) ÷ Difficulty Penalty

Intent Multiplier:
  - Transactional/Commercial = 1.5
  - Informational with tool intent = 1.0
  - Pure informational = 0.6

Difficulty Penalty:
  - KD 0–30  = 1.0  (we can win in 3 months)
  - KD 31–50 = 1.5  (6 months with great content)
  - KD 51–70 = 2.5  (12 months with backlinks)
  - KD 71–85 = 4.0  (18+ months, need DR 50+)
  - KD 86+   = 6.0  (don't bother early)
```

**This score tells us realistic 12-month revenue potential per tool**, not just raw traffic.

**These 7 USPs are non-negotiable.** They are the moat. They are why we will outrank Bankrate, NerdWallet, and Calculator.net on tools they've owned for 15 years.
